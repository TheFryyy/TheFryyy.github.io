Creep

As you may have understood, Creep is the project I am currently working on. It is an horror game. This is the story of a young videographer who lacks sensations and decided to buzz with a video. So he took the plane to Northfield Land a place where it is said that an american laboratory make test on humans. You are this character and you arrive to the Northfield Land airport.



For now, I mainly made modeling and texturing.
I coded the big panel to make it blink and I will soon code an
